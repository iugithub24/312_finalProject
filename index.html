Software Development

The CIT Web and Software development concentration builds on the foundations in the Computer and Information Technology program and prepares students for careers as programmers, systems analysts, website designers, mobile application developers, and cloud computing engineers. Students learn how to create cutting-edge software applications, manage projects, collaborate with their peers, generate and exchange data, and successfully interact with users.

Cybersecurity

Students in the CIT Information Security concentration are prepared for an in-demand career in the development, monitoring, and maintenance of secure systems. The concentration builds on the foundations in the Computer and Information Technology program and prepares students for careers as data security professionals, white hat hackers, project managers, and industry leaders in cybersecurity implementation and design.

Data Management

The CIT Data Management concentration builds on the foundations in the Computer and Information Technology program and prepares students for careers as data analysts, developers, and information science professionals.

Networking

Students in the CIT Networking Systems concentration learn how to design, build, and secure the networks on which modern life depends. The concentration builds on the foundations in the Computer and Information Technology program and prepares students for careers as network analysts and project managers.
